Last updated: May 30, 2026

URniversity ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Data We Collect

When signing in with Google, we receive the following from Google:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Google User ID (used solely to identify your account; never shared externally)

When signing in with email and password, we collect:

  • Email address (passwords are stored in encrypted form and cannot be read by us)

When using Guest Mode, all data is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to any server.

User-generated content created within the App, including:

  • Tasks, semester targets, future goals, inspirations, and journal entries
  • App settings (language, date format, semester configuration, etc.)

This content is linked to your account and stored in our database.

Feedback submissions: Feedback sent through the in-app feedback feature is anonymous. It contains no account information — only the feedback type, message text, and submission timestamp.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate and identify your account
  • To sync your content between your device and the cloud
  • To receive and review feedback in order to improve the App

We do not use your personal data for advertising and will never sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Supabase, a cloud database service. Supabase servers are located in the United States and operate under industry-standard security practices.

We enforce Row Level Security (RLS) policies to ensure that each user can only access their own data.

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Google Sign-In (Google LLC): Google Privacy Policy
  • Supabase (Supabase Inc.): Supabase Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You may permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time via Settings → Delete Account within the App. This action is irreversible.

Anonymous feedback data is not linked to any account, is not affected by account deletion, and contains no personally identifiable information.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us using the information below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
